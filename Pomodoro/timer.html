Welcome to the Pomodoro Timer!

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses a timer to break work down into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

Why Pomodoro?

  • Boosts Focus: Helps maintain concentration on a single task.
  • Reduces Burnout: Regular breaks prevent mental fatigue.
  • Enhances Productivity: Encourages efficient work habits.
  • Improves Time Awareness: Makes you more conscious of how you spend your time.

Words to Set Life to Discipline:

"Discipline is the bridge between goals and accomplishment." - Jim Rohn
