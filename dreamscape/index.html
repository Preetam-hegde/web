Idle Empire Builder v1.0

Welcome to Idle Empire Builder!

Build your own city and watch it grow! Click on buildings to purchase and upgrade them. Your city will generate income even when you're away.

Let's start with a quick tutorial to get you familiar with the game.

How to Play

Game Objective

Build and upgrade buildings to generate income and grow your city. The more buildings you have, the more money you earn automatically!

Buildings

Each building generates income over time. Upgrade buildings to increase their income. More expensive buildings generate more income.

Prestige

Once you've built a substantial city, you can reset your progress to earn prestige points. These points provide permanent bonuses to your income in future runs.

Automation

Your city generates income even when you're not actively playing. Come back later to collect your earnings!
